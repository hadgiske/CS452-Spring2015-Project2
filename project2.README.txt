Katie Hadgis & Luke Shaw, 4/26/2015

Use a,w,s,d to control diamond object and avoid boxes.
Hitting the boxes causes a game over. Click the New Game button that appears to restart the game.
Player recieves 5 points per half second with a maximum of three minutes game play.
Surviving the full three minutes constitutes a win for the player. The game ends and clicking the New Game button that appears restarts the game.

Flying boxes move in the 3 dimensions. The player moves in 2 dimensions. 
Objects cast shadow on ground plane which is displayed as a type of texture.
